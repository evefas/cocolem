WHAT IS IT ?

Deeply nourishing, soothing and moisturising buttery-goodness that restores dry, cracked lips, and perfectly conditions happy, healthy ones. Gently, exfoliating and moisturising lip saviour that buffs away dry, dead skin cells revealing plumper, softer and kissable lips.

2

LEMON DROP & SWEET ORANGE

Cocolem lip balms have been lovingly formulated with deeply moisturising avocado oil, creamy shea and mango butters, nourishing almond, orange seed and vitamin E oils, as well as vegan myrica fruit wax, soothing calendula and penetration enhancing sunflower lecithin to ensure your lips are properly hydrated, nourished and soft. Offering 100% natural moisture AND a barrier to lock that moisture in place = a bloody great lip balm.

3

INGREDIENTS

Myrica Cerifera Fruit (Myrica) Wax, Prunus Amygdalus Dulcis (Sweet Almond) Oil, Mangifera Indica Seed (Mango) Butter, Butyrospermum Parkii (Shea) Butter, Lecithin, Helianthus Annuus Seed (Sunflower) Oil, Citrus Aurantium Dulcis Seed (Orange) Oil, Persea Gratissima (Avocado) Oil, Beta- Carotene (Carrot Oil), Citrus Sinensis Peel (Orange) Oil Expressed, Tocopheryl (Vitamin E) Acetate, Calendula Officinalis Flower (Calendula) Extract, Citral,* Limonene*, Linalool* naturally occurring in essential oils

4

HOW TO USE IT

Use a clean, dry finger and gently warm the lip balm to release a powerhouse of moisturisation. Apply as often as required, definitely before bed, chapping weather conditions and underneath lipstick to stop your lips from drying out.
